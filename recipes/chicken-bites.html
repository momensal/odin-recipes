Chicken Bites

Description

Crispy golden chicken nuggets tossed in a fiery blend of sweet chili, sriracha, and creamy mayo, finished with a hit of fresh green onions. Easy, addictive, and built for dipping. This is comfort food with a spicy kick!

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces frozen crispy chicken nuggets
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha hot sauce, plus more if desired
  • 1/4 cup sliced green onions

Steps

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 390°F.
  2. Cook chicken nuggets in a single layer for about 10 minutes, shaking halfway. Work in batches if needed.
  3. Alternatively, bake at 400°F for about 12 minutes until golden.
  4. While nuggets cook, mix mayo, sweet chili sauce, and sriracha in a large bowl.
  5. Toss hot nuggets in the sauce until fully coated.
  6. Top with green onions and serve.
